Beans-to-Bars

For over three generations we have been the only company to produce chocolate in Ottawa. Production starts with ethically sourced cocoa beans—we combine 63% cocoa powder with cocoa butter. Fresh cream is added with sugar to create a rich creamy chocolate crumb. The crumb is added to butter and cocoa liquor to create the perfect chocolate mixture. The last process is to refine and temper the chocolates creating the perfect shine before they are sent to your homes.
