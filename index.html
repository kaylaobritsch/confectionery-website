    Chocolate Life

Beans-to-Bars

For over three generations we have been the only company to produce chocolate in Ottawa. Production starts with ethically sourced cocoa beans—we then combine 63% cocoa powder with cocoa butter. Fresh cream is added with sugar to create a rich creamy chocolate crumb. The crumb is added to butter and cocoa liquor to create the perfect chocolate mixture. This chocolate mixture is used to create everything from cake pops to chocolate bars and then sent to your home.
